---
question: "Can I use kemo iptv on multiple devices simultaneously?"
category: "technical"
order: 6
---

Yes, kemo iptv allows users to access the service on multiple devices. Depending on the subscription package chosen, you may stream simultaneously on one or more devices. This multi-device capability is great for families or homes with multiple users wanting to watch different content at the same time, thereby maximizing the service's value.
