---
question: "Can Kemo IPTV be used on multiple devices simultaneously?"
category: "technical"
order: 6
---

Yes, Kemo IPTV allows multi-device usage, making it ideal for families and individuals who wish to access content from different devices at the same time. Depending on your subscription plan, you may be able to connect and stream on multiple devices simultaneously. This flexibility ensures that each member of your household can enjoy their preferred content on their device without any interruptions.
