---
question: "Can I use kemo iptv on multiple devices simultaneously?"
category: "technical"
order: 6
---

Yes, Kemo IPTV allows users to stream content on multiple devices, but it depends on the subscription package selected. Some packages may offer multi-device streaming as a standard feature, allowing households to enjoy different channels on various devices at the same time. Users interested in this capability should check the terms of their subscription plan or opt for an upgrade to enable multi-device usage if needed.
